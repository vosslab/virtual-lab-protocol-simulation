OQ-5: Flask Variant Choice

Three T-75 flask design variants have been created. Pick the preferred design for deployment. All three support liquid fill and labeling via the same rendering system.

v1: Current Default

Angled Neck, Rectangular Body

Flat rectangular body with angled neck. Classic simple design. Default in current build.

v2: Vented Modern

Slanted Neck, Vented Cap

Modern design with slanted neck and visible vent holes. Emphasizes gas exchange in culture.

v3: Classic Corning

Straight Neck, Tall Body

Classic Corning-style design. Straight cylindrical neck, tapered body. Traditional, iconic appearance.
